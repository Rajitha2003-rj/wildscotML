About Us

At WildScot Photography, we believe in capturing the untamed beauty of Scotland in every frame. Led by Malcolm Lismore, a passionate freelance photographer with a deep love for the natural world, our work is a celebration of the rugged landscapes, diverse wildlife, and the unique moments that make life extraordinary. Based on the North West coast of Scotland, Malcolm's lens brings to life the dramatic scenery and serene moments that define this breathtaking region. Whether it's a majestic mountain, a fleeting moment with coastal birds, or the joy of a wedding day, our goal is to create images that tell a story and resonate with emotion. Welcome to a world where nature's beauty meets timeless artistry.

Landscape Photography

Malcolm's landscape photography captures the raw, untouched beauty of Scotland's rugged terrains. From the misty Highlands to the serene coastal cliffs, his images bring the majesty of the natural world to life, inviting viewers to experience the breathtaking scenery as if they were there.

Wildlife Photography

With a keen eye for detail and patience, Malcolm specializes in wildlife photography, particularly Scotland's diverse birdlife and coastal creatures. His ability to capture the essence of these animals in their natural habitats reflects both his technical skill and deep respect for the environment.

Wedding Photography

Malcolm's approach to wedding photography is all about storytelling. He blends the natural beauty of the Scottish landscape with the joy and emotion of your special day, creating timeless images that capture not just moments, but memories that last a lifetime.

Portrait Photography

In his portrait work, Malcolm combines the tranquility of nature with the personality of his subjects, crafting unique and evocative images. Whether in a forest, by the sea, or amidst the mountains, his portraits reveal the true spirit of the individuals he photographs.
